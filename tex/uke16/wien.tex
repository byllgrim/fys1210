\paragraph{Negativ} \mbox{} \\
Den negative tilbakekoblingen kontrollerer closed loop gain $A_{CL}$.
Feedback loopen gir en forsterkning, men diodene begrenser spenningen.
$$A_{CL} = \frac{R_f}{R_{inn}} + 1
         = \frac{R_3 + R_4}{R_5} + 1$$
